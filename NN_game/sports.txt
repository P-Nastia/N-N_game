football
The worlds most popular team sport. The major governing body is FIFA, currently present in over 200 countries worldwide with international competitions.
basketball
The game is generally played by two five-player sides that pass the ball around to score baskets within predetermined hoops. 
volleyball
Requires two sides of six players to hit a larger ball across the net suspended within an indoor court.
golf
This involves a set of clubs and balls with professional players aiming to hit the ball in fewer shots across an expansive field or course.
badminton
Is a racquet sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net.
hockey
Is a team sport played on ice skates, usually on an ice skating rink with lines and markings specific to the sport.
parkour
Is an athletic training discipline or sport in which practitioners attempt to get from point A to point B in the fastest and most efficient way possible.
bobsleigh
A sport in which you race down a track covered in ice in a small vehicle with long metal blades under it
kayaking
Is a fun activity that involves moving through water in a small water vessel with the aid of a double-bladed paddle.
archery
Is the sport that involves using a bow to shoot arrows.
